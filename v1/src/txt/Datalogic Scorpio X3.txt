------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 COLDBOOT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Turn off the Device
 2  Remove Battery
 3  Simultanously press RESET and SCAN
 4  Reinsert the Battery and boot


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 FACTORY RESET
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Perform COLD BOOT
     Turn off the Device
     Remove Battery
     Simultanously press RESET and SCAN
     Reinsert the Battery and boot
 2  Turn on the Device
 3  Press and hold ESC and 0 keys
 4  Confirm with ENT
 5  The device will perform a CLEAN BOOT
