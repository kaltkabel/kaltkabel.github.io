 COLDBOOT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Press ESC + ENTER + POWER


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 FACTORY RESET
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Perform a COLD BOOT
     Press ESC + ENT + POWER
 2  Quickly release the keys when the screen goes black
 3  Within 1 second press and hold down ESC + "0" keys
     Release the keys when a confirmation window shows up
 4  Confirm with "1"


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 OS UPDATE
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Copy DLUpdate.bin ----> \MemorX3\Backup
 2  Copy DLUPDEX_Run.exe -> \MemorX3\Backup
 3  Start DLUPDEX_Run.exe from the MemorX3
 4  Perform Factory Reset
