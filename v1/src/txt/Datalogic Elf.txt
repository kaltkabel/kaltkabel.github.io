------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 COLDBOOT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Remove Battery
 2  Press the RESET button
 3  Reinsert the Battery
 4  Turn on the system


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 FACTORY RESET
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Perform a COLD BOOT
     Remove Battery
     Press the RESET button
     Reinsert the Battery
 2  Immediately press and hold RED phone key + ZERO key
 3  Release the keys when the message "Clean Boot has been detected" appears
 4  Confirm with ENTER
 5  The System will begin the FACTORY RESET
