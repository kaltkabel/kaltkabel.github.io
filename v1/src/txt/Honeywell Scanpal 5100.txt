 COLDBOOT CE
-------------
1. Press and immediatly release POWER and the RESET button
2. The Device will successfully perform a COLD BOOT if "COLD BOOT (H/W) RESET" is shown



 FACTORY RESET CE
------------------
Instruct Bootloader to FACTORY RESET:
1. Perform a Cold Boot
2. When the "COLD (H/W) RESET" displays:
     Enter: 1 > 2 > 7 > 7
3. When a Menu displays:
     Enter: 1 > 2 > ENTER
4. The device will first perform a RAM TEST
4. After the Test the device will perform a FACTORY RESET
