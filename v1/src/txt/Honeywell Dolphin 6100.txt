 COLDBOOT
----------
1. Press and hold the RESET button (with stylus)
2. Press and release the POWER button
3. Release the RESET button
OR
1. Hold the POWER button until a menu appears
2. Select HARD RESET


 FACTORY RESET WEH
-------------------
Copy Files
1. Connect the Device to the Computer
2. Powertools and Demos -> IPSM/Honeywell/Autoinstall

Instruct Bootloader to FACTORY RESET:
1. Perform a Hard Reset / Cold boot (Power Menu)
2. When the Notification LED flashes:
	 Enter on numeric keyboard: 1 > 2 > 7 > 7
3. Confirm with Enter > Enter
