 COLDBOOT WEH
--------------
1. Turn on the Device
2. Simultanously press BLUE key and GREEN phone key or CTRL+ESC
3. The Device will reboot and will perform a COLD BOOT



 FACTORY RESET WEH
-------------------
Copy Files
1. Connect the Device to the Computer
2. Powertools and Demos -> IPSM/Honeywell/Autoinstall

Instruct Bootloader to FACTORY RESET:
1. Cold Boot (BLUE Key + GREEN phone Key or CTRL+ESC)
2. When the screen turns on:
	 Enter on normal keyboard:     1 > 2 > 7 > 7 (NUM)
	 or
	 Enter on calculator keyboard: 7 > 8 > 1 > 1
3. Choose the first Option:
     Enter "1" or ENT
	 Confirm with "Y" or with ENT
4. The Device reboots
