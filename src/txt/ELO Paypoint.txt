-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ANDROID Accessing the Android Home Menu                                                 ANDROID
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Start EloView
 2  Go to the Home tab
 3  Press on the white Background 5 times
 4  Select Android Home

 Info: You will be booted to Android Home every time device is rebooted after this!
       To return to EloView, Factory Reset the device!
