WEH Coldboot                                                                                 WEH
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Remove the BATTERY
 2  Press the RESET button (left button)
 3  Insert the BATTERY


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WEH Factory Reset                                                                            WEH
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Perform a COLD BOOT
 2  Instruct Bootloader to CLEAN BOOT
     >OPTION 1:
       1  Press and hold POWER + VOL DOWN (the lower button on the right side)
       2  Release the button when "Release IO" appears
       3  Confirm with VOL UP
     >OPTION 2:
       1  Press and hold POWER button
       2  Release the button when "Release IO" appears
       3  Confirm with 1 key -> 2 key -> 1 key -> 2key
	 
	 
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------	 
WEH OS Upgrade / Update                                                                      WEH
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Copy AutoFlash.img -> microSD:\
 2  Insert the microSD
 3  Perform a COLD BOOT
     Remove BATTERY
     Press RESET (left button)
     Insert BATTERY
 4  When "AutoFlash Complete" appears, remove the microSD and restart

ANDROID Accessing the Recovery Menu                                                      ANDROID
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 > USB Debugging ADB method
    1  Connect the device to PC via USB Debugging
    2  Send command: adb device recovery
 > Button combination method
    1  Reboot the device
    2  Press and hold Vol+ and Vol- (the buttons are located on the right site)
    3  Select Recovery
	
 Recovery Menu (as seen in FW 6.0)
  > Reboot system now
  > Weipe data/factory reset
  > Reboot to bootloader
  > Apply update from ADB
  > Apply update from SD card
  > Apply update from IPSM
  > Wipe cache partition
  > Wipe ipsm partition
  > Mount /system
  > View recovery logs
  > Power off
