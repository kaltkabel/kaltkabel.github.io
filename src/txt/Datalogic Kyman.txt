------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 COLDBOOT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Turn on the Device
 2  Press "F1" + "F2" + SCAN + POWER


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 FACTORY RESET
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Turn on the Device
 2  Perform a COLD BOOT
     Press "F1" + "F2" + SCAN + POWER
     Release all keys when the screen goes black
 3  Immediately press and hold ESC and "0"
 4  Release the keys when the left orange LED starts blinking
 5  Confirm with "1"
