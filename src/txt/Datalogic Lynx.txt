------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 COLDBOOT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Turn off the Device
 2  Remove Battery
 3  Press the RESET button
 4  Reinsert the Battery and boot


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 FACTORY RESET
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Perform a WARM BOOT
     Press and hold YELLOW key + BLUE key +  RED phone key
 2  Before the splash screen appears:
     Press and hold "0" key + RED phone key
 3  Confirm with ENTER
