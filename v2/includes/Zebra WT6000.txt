----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ANDROID Coldboot                                                                       ANDROID
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Simultaneously press and hold the following buttons for 5 seconds
     > Power button
     > P1 button
     > P2 button
 2  Release the buttons when the device reboots


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ANDROID Accessing the Recovery Menu                                                    ANDROID
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1  Reboot the device
 2  Hold down the P1 button
 3  Release when the device vibrates
 
 Recovery Menu (as seen in FW 5.1.1)
  > Reboot system now
  > Apply update from emulated storage
  > Apply update from ADB
  > Power down
